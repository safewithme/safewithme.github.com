Our Mission:
Protect your Privacy

  • SafeWith.me is your portable safe. It lets you store and share files with End-to-End encryption on any device via HTML5.

  • Documents are encrypted with your personal key before uploading, using the trusted and well-known OpenPGP standard (more info).

  • No one (not even we) can read your documents without your private key. It's generated on your computer and belongs only to you.

  • Share files securely with others using their personal key.

  • Your documents are available on your device even when you're offline, using the newest HTML5 technologies.

  • No need to register or provide any personal information. Just login with your existing Google Account.

  • It's completely Open Source. Providing transparency of the software and enabling collaboration with a community of security experts.
