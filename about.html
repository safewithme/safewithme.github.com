"So every online storage provider tells me they're
secure... what makes you any better?"

Because SafeWith.me gives you total control of your privacy.

  • Documents are encrypted in your web browser with your personal key before uploading, using the trusted and well-known OpenPGP standard.
  • Other storage services on the web may encrypt your data with their key after upload. But this means that they will still always have access. This presents a very real privacy threat for you, if their service is compromised.
  • With SafeWith.me, no one (not even we) can read your documents without your private key, since only the encrypted versions of your files and file-metadata are sent to our servers. You are the only one who is able to access your data.
  • Login using your existing Google Account means you don't have to register to yet another service or provide any personal information.
  • Secure sharing of documents with others using their personal key enables only the recipients to have access.
  • Your documents are available on your device even when you're offline, using the newest HTML5 technologies.
  • It's Open Source. Providing transparency of the software and enabling collaboration with a community of security experts.
